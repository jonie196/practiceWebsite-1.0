About

Me

His work is focused on aligning Body, Mind, Heart and Soul by supporting to align the energy flow in the whole energetic system ~ Kundalini and the Chakras. This can happen through trauma healing, resolving destructive patterns or through spiritual activation, meditation and specific practices that support the overall alignment that is needed to support the evolution of the person and the soul in a balanced and integrated way.

Jonas opened up to spirituality very early in his life and since then is 110% committed to the Path of Self Realisation, Awakening and Healing. His gift is it to Read, See and Perceive energies very clearly - giving him deep Insights, Wisdom and understanding about deep and complex psychological, emotional and spiritual human aspects.
